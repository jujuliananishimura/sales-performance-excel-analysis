# Sales Analysis Project 

## Project Overview
This project analyzes historical sales data to identify trends, product performance, and the relationship between sales and discounts.  
The goal is to extract actionable business insights using pivot tables and visualizations.

The analysis was conducted using **Google Sheets**, focusing on clarity, accuracy, and business-oriented storytelling.

---

## Dataset
The dataset includes the following fields:

- Product  
- Quantity  
- Price  
- Sales  
- MSRP  
- Order Date  
- Month-Year  
- Discount  
- Discount %  

Data was cleaned before analysis (numeric formatting standardized, percentages corrected, and date fields validated).

---

## Key Analysis & Pivot Tables

### 1️⃣ Total Sales by Product
- Identifies top-performing product categories.
- Helps prioritize high-revenue products.

### 2️⃣ Sales Trend Over Time (Month-Year)
- Tracks sales performance across months and years.
- Highlights seasonality and growth patterns.

### 3️⃣ Average Discount by Product
- Compares discount strategies across product categories.
- Identifies products with higher reliance on discounts.

### 4️⃣ Sales vs Discount Analysis
- Evaluates the relationship between average discounts and total sales.
- Helps assess whether higher discounts lead to higher revenue.

---

## Key Insights
- Certain products consistently generate higher total sales, indicating strong market demand.
- Sales show noticeable fluctuations over time, suggesting seasonality effects.
- Products with higher average discounts do not always produce higher total sales.
- Strategic discounting appears more effective for specific product categories.

---

## Business Takeaways
- Focus marketing and inventory efforts on top-performing products.
- Optimize discount strategies rather than applying uniform discounts.
- Use historical sales trends to support demand forecasting and planning.
